DMIT – Dermatoglyphics Multiple Intelligence Test

DMIT is a scientific assessment that analyzes fingerprint patterns to understand a person's natural talents, intelligence types, and learning style.

Fingerprints develop before birth and remain unchanged throughout life. By studying these patterns, DMIT helps identify the unique strengths and potential of every individual.

Benefits of DMIT

  • Discover natural intelligence types
  • Understand learning styles
  • Identify hidden talents
  • Support better career guidance
  • Help parents guide children effectively
